The Project

The "Eighteenth-Century Letter-writers" project is a digital initiative dedicated to preserving and exploring the world of 18th-century English correspondence. Our goal is to make these invaluable historical documents accessible to researchers and enthusiasts alike.

Filter Search

Refine your results with intuitive filters. Narrow down thousands of documents to exactly what you need with just a few clicks.

Advanced Search

Uncover specific letters or data points with powerful, multi-parameter queries. Search by author, recipient, date, location, and more.
